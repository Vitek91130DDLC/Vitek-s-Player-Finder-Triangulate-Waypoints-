# Vitek's Player Finder (Triangulate Waypoints)

**Vitek's Player Finder** is a clever utility mod designed to help you locate distant players, structures, or landmarks using the real-world mathematical principle of **triangulation**. 

Instead of running thousands of blocks to find out where a base or a player is, you can now pinpoint their exact coordinates from a distance by taking just two directional sights!

---

## ✨ Features
* **True Triangulation:** Calculate exact coordinates of distant targets using geometric intersection.
* **Seamless Map Integration:** Automatically creates a waypoint at the intersection point.
* **Fully Configurable:** Easily change the hotkey in your standard Minecraft controls menu.
* **Perfect for SMP & Anarchy:** Ideal for tracking down other players, hunting bases, or mapping out distant structures safely.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works (Step-by-Step)

Using the mod is simple, but requires a small change in your position to calculate the intersection:

1. **First Sight (Point A):** Look directly at your target (or the direction where you think a player/base is) and press the **"V"** key (default). This casts the first imaginary line.
2. **Move Away:** Walk a reasonable distance to the left or right to create an angle (this forms your baseline).
3. **Second Sight (Point B):** Look at the *same* target from your new position and press **"V"** again. 
4. **Boom, Waypoint!** The mod will automatically calculate where those two lines intersect and instantly create a Xaero's waypoint at that exact spot.

## 🚀 Requirements & Compatibility

To function properly, this mod requires a map system to plot the coordinates. **You must install the following mods for this to work:**

* **Fabric API**
* [Xaero's Minimap](https://modrinth.com/mod/xaeros-minimap)
* [Xaero's World Map](https://modrinth.com/mod/xaeros-world-map)

*Currently compatible with **Fabric 1.21.1**.*
